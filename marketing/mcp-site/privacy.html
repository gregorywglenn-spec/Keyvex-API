Privacy Policy

Effective May 10, 2026. Last updated May 10, 2026.

KeyVex provides a Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that exposes U.S. public financial-disclosure data (SEC filings, congressional trades, federal contracts, lobbying disclosures, and similar government-published records) to AI agents and developers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data.

Plain-English summary: we collect the minimum data needed to run the service (your email, an API key, and request logs). We do not sell your data. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking. We share data only with the service providers who help us operate the platform (Google Cloud, Stripe, etc.) and only when we have to.

1. Who we are

KeyVex is a software service operated by the team behind keyvex.com and mcp.keyvex.com. The operating legal entity is in the process of formation; once established, this section will be updated with the entity's full name, jurisdiction, and registered address.

KeyVex is a data publisher, not a registered investment adviser. The records we surface come from primary government sources (SEC EDGAR, the U.S. Senate Office of Public Records, the U.S. House Clerk, USAspending.gov, and similar public registries). We do not provide investment advice and we do not generate proprietary financial recommendations.

2. What we collect

We collect three categories of data, all of them limited:

Account information

  • Email address. Required for signup, account recovery, and operational notices (e.g., service-status emails, security alerts).
  • API key. A unique secret we issue when you sign up, used to authenticate your requests to the MCP server. You can rotate or revoke it at any time.
  • Display name (optional). If you choose to provide one for support correspondence.

Usage data

  • Request logs. For each MCP request, we record which tool was called, the request timestamp, the originating IP address, response status, and (for paid tiers) the byte count of the response.
  • Aggregate metrics. Counts of requests per day, per endpoint, used for capacity planning and billing.

Payment information (paid tiers only)

  • If you subscribe to a paid plan, we use Stripe to process payments. Stripe collects your name, billing address, and payment-method details directly. KeyVex does not store credit-card numbers, bank-account numbers, or full payment-method details on our servers. We receive only a Stripe customer identifier and the metadata Stripe surfaces to merchants (last four digits, card brand, etc.).

We do not collect: contents of any documents you upload (we don't accept uploads), tracking pixels or third-party ad-network identifiers, contact-list data, behavioral profiles across other sites, or any sensitive personal information (race, religion, health, sexual orientation, etc.).

3. Why we collect it

  • Deliver the service. Email + API key are required to authenticate your MCP requests.
  • Bill paid customers. Request logs feed into quota and overage calculations.
  • Detect abuse. IP-level rate limiting and anomaly detection to protect the service from misuse.
  • Operational notices. Email you about service incidents, breaking changes, or material policy updates.
  • Improve the product. Aggregate usage metrics tell us which tools agents use most, where queries time out, and where to invest in performance.

We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not sell, rent, lease, or barter your data to any third party for any purpose. Period.

4. Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers that help us run KeyVex:

  • Google Cloud / Firebase (United States) — hosts the MCP server, the Firestore database where account and log data live, and the Hosting platform that serves keyvex.com.
  • Stripe, Inc. (United States) — payment processing for paid-tier subscribers.
  • Email-delivery provider (TBD; will be one of Postmark, SendGrid, or Resend) — operational and billing emails.
  • Law enforcement / regulators — only when required by law (a valid subpoena, court order, or similar legal process). We will challenge overly broad requests.

All service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data-processing agreements. We do not authorize them to use your data for their own marketing or product purposes.

We do not share your account or usage data with Anthropic, OpenAI, or any other AI model provider. The MCP protocol is direct between your client (e.g., Claude Desktop) and our server; the AI model provider only sees the responses you relay through your client, not your KeyVex account credentials.

5. How long we keep it

  • Account data (email, API key, display name) — retained for as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days after you close your account, except where we are legally required to retain longer (e.g., tax or accounting records).
  • Request logs — retained for up to 13 months (rolling) for billing reconciliation and abuse detection, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Aggregate metrics — retained indefinitely in de-identified form (counts of requests, no per-user attribution).
  • Payment records — retained as required by applicable tax law, typically 7 years.

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regardless of where you live:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction. Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion. Close your account and request that we delete your personal data (subject to the retention rules above for legal compliance).
  • Export. Request a machine-readable copy of your account data and usage history.
  • Opt out of marketing email. Every promotional email we send (if any) includes an unsubscribe link. Operational and security emails cannot be opted out while you have an active account.

Residents of California (CCPA / CPRA), the European Economic Area (GDPR), the United Kingdom (UK GDPR), and other jurisdictions with comparable privacy laws have the additional rights granted by those laws, including the right to lodge a complaint with a data-protection authority. Contact us at the address below to exercise any of these rights and we will respond within the timelines required by applicable law (typically 30 to 45 days).

7. Cookies and tracking

The KeyVex marketing site (keyvex.com) does not set tracking cookies and does not run third-party analytics, ad networks, or fingerprinting scripts. The MCP API endpoint (mcp.keyvex.com) is a JSON API and does not use browser cookies.

A future authenticated dashboard (when launched) will use a single first-party session cookie strictly for keeping you logged in. No cross-site or behavioral tracking will be added without an updated Privacy Policy and, where required, your prior consent.

8. Data security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures:

  • All traffic to keyvex.com and mcp.keyvex.com uses TLS encryption.
  • API keys are stored hashed in our database; we cannot recover a key after issuance — only rotate it.
  • Production credentials are stored in Google Secret Manager with audit logging.
  • Access to production systems is limited to the engineering team and is logged.

No internet-connected service can be 100% secure. If we ever experience a security incident that affects your data, we will notify you in line with applicable breach-notification laws.

9. Children's privacy

KeyVex is a developer tool for accessing financial-disclosure data. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

KeyVex is operated from the United States. If you access the service from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to, processed, and stored in the U.S. (and any other country where our service providers operate). By using the service you consent to that transfer.

Where required (e.g., for users in the EEA or UK), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other valid transfer mechanisms with our service providers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Effective and Last updated dates at the top of this page reflect the most recent changes. Material changes (i.e., changes that affect what we collect, how we use it, or who we share it with) will be communicated to active account holders by email at least 30 days before they take effect.

Continued use of the service after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. If you do not agree to the updated policy, you may close your account before it takes effect.

12. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy, or to exercise any of the rights described above:

  • Email: privacy@keyvex.com (once configured; in the meantime use contact@capitaledge.app)
  • Web: keyvex.com

We will respond to verified requests within 30 days, or sooner if required by applicable law.
